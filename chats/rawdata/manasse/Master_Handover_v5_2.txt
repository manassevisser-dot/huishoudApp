## INI-PLAN (Stap-voor-stap)

1. Open Excel (.xlsm) en VBE (Alt+F11).
2. Voeg modules toe: Utils, FormBackground, FormHelpers, FormLayout, LayoutSpecsHelpers, FormData, FormEvents, frmBuilders, MonthlySheet, Navigation, Reporting, TableSetup.
3. Voeg klassen toe: CImgEvents, CBtnEvents, CCmbEvents, CTxtEvents.
4. Voeg UserForms toe: frmHuishouden, frmVast (MultiPage), frmInvoer, frmDebugDashboard.
5. Compileer (Debug → Compile VBAProject).
6. Test via frmDebugDashboard (Smoke Tests, Sanity-check).
