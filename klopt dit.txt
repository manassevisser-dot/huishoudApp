- **Laag**: Architecture & Governance (overkoepelend)

## 🔧 Fix 4: Eventnaam RFC-compliant maken

**Naming convention voor events:**
```
[domain].[action]_[qualifier]  →  snake_case, lowercase
Voorbeelden:
- csv.parse_success
- validation.boundary_failed  
- feature.undo_not_implemented
- state.transition_invalid
```
